The grammar includes all noun declinations in Latvian as well as singular and plural word forms 
in the nominatīvs, ģenitīvs, datīvs, akuzatīvs, instrumentālis un lokatīvs cases.

Words included in the lexicon:
I declination - krēsls, krīts, prāts
II declination - ūdens, skapis, brālis, gulbis, kurvis
III declination - medus, ledus, lietus
IV declination - māja, diena, vara
V declination - laime, krūze, klase
VI declination - pils, sirds, nakts

Additional rules are implemented for the II declination for the singular ģenitīvs case and all plural cases:
b -> bj
n -> ņ
l -> ļ
p -> pj
v -> vj

Exceptions:
ūdenis -> ūdens (II declination singular nominatīvs)
ūdeņa -> ūdens (II declination singular ģenitīvs)

Output cleanup:
The "^" symbol gets deleted (to improve readability)
The "_" symbol gets replaced with a space (for the ar prefix in the instrumentālis case)
